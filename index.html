Our Story

The H.A. Models Legacy

Born in Tangier, H.A. Models is driven by a passion for Moroccan craftsmanship. Since 2026, we collaborate with the kingdom's finest artisans to create pieces that blend tradition and modernity.

Our Artisans

The Craftsmanship

Each piece is the result of meticulous work by our artisans. Embroiderers, weavers, and goldsmiths perpetuate ancestral techniques to bring unique creations to life.
